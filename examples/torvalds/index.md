# Engineering Style Wiki

Generated by [gitstyle](https://github.com/edgarpavlovsky/gitstyle) from the public commit history of [torvalds](https://github.com/torvalds).

The Linux kernel is one of the most distinctive codebases in existence: 30+ million lines of C maintained by thousands of contributors under the direct oversight of a single technical authority. Its style conventions are not arbitrary preferences but load-bearing engineering decisions shaped by decades of operating at extreme scale.
