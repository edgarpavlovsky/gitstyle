## Filtering

- Merge commits were excluded
- Commits modifying only lockfiles or generated code were excluded
- Commits with fewer than 3 changed lines were excluded
- Bot commits (dependabot, renovate) were excluded

After filtering, **731 commits** were used for analysis.

## Confidence Scores

Confidence scores on individual wiki articles are derived from:
- **Number of supporting commits**: More evidence = higher confidence
- **Consistency**: Patterns that appear across multiple repositories score higher
- **Recency**: Recent commits are weighted more heavily than older ones

| Confidence | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `high` | Pattern observed in 10+ commits across 2+ repositories |
| `medium` | Pattern observed in 5-9 commits or concentrated in a single repository |
| `low` | Pattern observed in fewer than 5 commits, may be situational |
